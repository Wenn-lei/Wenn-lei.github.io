My research interest focus on solid mechanics and computational mechanics.

Research Overview

Fracture toughness of lattice materials

We are interested in the modeling of the mechanical behaviors of lattice materials.

Phase-field modeling

We are interested in phase-field modeling for fracture mechanics.

Multiwalled Boron Nitride Nanotubes

We modeled the radial compression process of MWBNNTs using molecular dynamics.

Hyperelastic materials

We are interested in modeling the mechanical behaviors of hyperelastic materials.
